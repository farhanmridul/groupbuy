Shipping & Returns Policy

Everything you need to know about shipping, delivery, returns, and refunds at GroupBuy.

Shipping Policy

Shipping Timeframes

GroupBuy operates on a unique model where shipping only begins after a product reaches its Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ). Each product page displays an estimated shipping date once MOQ is reached.

Shipping Carriers

We ship via major carriers including UPS, FedEx, and USPS. International shipments may use additional carriers depending on destination.

Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight, dimensions, and destination. We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days)
  • Expedited Shipping (3-5 business days)
  • Express Shipping (1-2 business days)

Free standard shipping is available for orders over $100 within the continental United States.

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, and fees that may apply. These charges are not included in your order total and will be collected by the shipping carrier.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also view order status and tracking information in your account dashboard.

Returns & Refunds

Return Policy

GroupBuy accepts returns on most items within 30 days of delivery. To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In the original, unused condition
  • In the original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase

Non-Returnable Items

Some items cannot be returned due to health, safety, or other reasons:

  • Personal care items
  • Products with broken seals
  • Items marked as non-returnable on the product page
  • Digital products or downloadable software

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log into your account
  2. Navigate to your order history
  3. Select the order containing the item you wish to return
  4. Click "Return Item" and follow the instructions

Return Shipping Costs

For returns due to:

  • Defective items or our error: We'll provide a prepaid return shipping label and refund your full purchase price.
  • Change of mind or customer preference: Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility, and a restocking fee of 5% may apply.

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll notify you about the status of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed within 3-5 business days. Depending on your payment method and financial institution, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

Group Buy Specific Policies

Pre-MOQ Cancellations

If you decide to cancel your order before the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) is reached:

  • Your 5% deposit will be fully refunded to your original payment method
  • No cancellation fees apply
  • Processing time for the refund is typically 3-5 business days

Post-MOQ Cancellations

If you decide to cancel your order after the MOQ has been reached but before shipping:

  • Your 5% deposit is non-refundable
  • The remaining balance will be refunded if already paid
  • Exceptions may be made for extenuating circumstances at our discretion

MOQ Not Reached

If a product fails to reach its MOQ within the specified timeframe:

  • Your 5% deposit will be automatically refunded in full
  • You'll receive an email notification
  • No action is required on your part

Questions About Your Order?

Our customer service team is here to help with any questions about shipping, returns, or refunds.
