How GroupBuy Works

GroupBuy allows you to access wholesale prices by joining other customers to meet minimum order quantities (MOQ).

The GroupBuy Process

Our unique model brings wholesale prices to everyday consumers through group purchasing power.

1. Browse Products

Find products you're interested in purchasing at wholesale prices.

2. Pay a 5% Deposit

Reserve your spot with a small 5% deposit of the wholesale price.

3. Wait for MOQ

When enough customers join, the minimum order quantity is met.

4. Get Your Product

Pay the remaining balance and receive your product at wholesale price!

Benefits of GroupBuy

  • Save up to 50% off retail prices
  • Access products normally only available to businesses
  • No risk - deposit refunded if MOQ isn't reached
  • Track progress toward minimum order quantities
  • High-quality products from verified suppliers

Frequently Asked Questions

If the minimum order quantity isn't reached within the specified timeframe, your deposit will be fully refunded.

Once the MOQ is reached, products typically ship within 1-3 weeks. Each product page shows an estimated shipping date.

You can cancel your order and receive a full refund before the MOQ is reached. After the MOQ is reached, cancellations are subject to a 5% fee.
