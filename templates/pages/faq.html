Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GroupBuy's group purchasing model, ordering process, shipping, and more.

General Questions

GroupBuy is an e-commerce platform that allows consumers to access wholesale prices by joining group purchases. When enough customers (the Minimum Order Quantity, or MOQ) join a single purchase, everyone gets the wholesale price instead of retail price.

1. Browse for products you're interested in.
2. Pay a 5% deposit to join the waitlist for a product.
3. Once enough people join the waitlist to meet the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ), the order is fulfilled.
4. You'll pay the remaining balance and receive your product at the wholesale price.
For more details, check out our How It Works page.

Savings vary by product, but customers typically save 30-50% off retail prices. Each product page clearly shows both the retail market price and the wholesale price you'll pay through GroupBuy.

Yes! GroupBuy ships to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times are calculated at checkout.

Ordering Process

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) is the minimum number of products that must be ordered for the manufacturer or supplier to fulfill the order at wholesale prices. For example, if a product has an MOQ of 100, we need at least 100 people to join the waitlist before the order can be placed.

Each product page shows a live progress bar indicating the current number of orders versus the required MOQ. You can see exactly how many more orders are needed before the batch is fulfilled.

If a product doesn't reach its MOQ within the specified timeframe, your 5% deposit will be fully refunded. There's no risk to joining a waitlist.

Yes, you can cancel your order anytime before the MOQ is reached, and your deposit will be fully refunded. Once the MOQ is reached, cancellations are subject to a 5% fee (your initial deposit).

Payment & Shipping

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are secure and encrypted.

You're only charged the 5% deposit when you join a waitlist. The remaining balance is charged only after the MOQ is reached and before shipping. We'll notify you when it's time to complete your payment.

Shipping times vary by product and destination. After the MOQ is reached, most domestic orders ship within 1-3 weeks. International shipping may take longer. Each product page displays an estimated shipping date after MOQ is reached.

Yes, once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number by email and can also view tracking information in your account dashboard.

Returns & Refunds

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most products. Items must be in original, unused condition with all packaging intact. For more details, please refer to our Shipping & Returns page.

To initiate a return, log into your account, go to your order history, select the order, and click "Return Item." Follow the instructions to complete the return process.

For returns due to defects or errors on our part, we'll cover return shipping costs. For returns due to customer preference, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs.

Once we receive your return, refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days. It may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your payment method and financial institution.
